Privacy Policy for Visitor Management System (VMS App)

Last Updated: 23 November 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information when you use the Visitor Management System (“VMS App”).

1. Information We Collect

  • Mobile Number: Used for verification and visitor identification.
  • Truecaller Data (If user chooses): Name and phone number fetched via Truecaller SDK.
  • Visitor Details: Name, company, email, purpose of visit, check-in/out time.
  • Device Information: Android version, device ID, logs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • For visitor verification and check-in/out.
  • To improve app performance, security, and support.
  • To notify hosts about visitor arrivals.
  • For maintaining visitor logs as per company policy.

3. Sharing of Information

We do NOT sell or trade your information. We may share certain data only with:

  • Organization administrators using the VMS system.
  • Government authorities if legally required.

4. Data Security

We follow encryption, secure APIs, and restricted database access to protect user data.

5. Third-Party SDKs

The app uses the Truecaller SDK for quick identity verification. By continuing, users agree to Truecaller’s privacy policy.

6. Your Rights

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request correction of your profile
  • Request access to stored data
